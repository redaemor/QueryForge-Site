Overview

This app is designed with self-employed, small business builders, plumbers, and similar tradespeople in mind. The general idea is:

  • A call, email, or social media DM comes through asking for work.
  • The user fills out the details of the customer with a new enquiry.
  • After the enquiry has been filled out with the requirements, the user compiles a quote accordingly and saves it.
  • The most notable part is that when the quote is saved the user will be reminded to follow up with the customer after X days. This is the easiest part for small businesses to miss.
  • Optionally, after the end user has been reminded about the quote after N times, a discount can be factored into the quote automatically. The discount amount and quantity of reminders are configurable.
  • The user can mark the quote as won, make modifications, delay the reminder, among other options.
  • This app was written with support by AI, otherwise this would take exponentially longer to code.
  • If you uninstall the app, all data will be lost unless you export the encrypted backup first and then re-import it.

Every step is designed to be as self-explanatory as possible. If you need to clarify what something is or does, or how to do something in the app, then I need to modify the app to make it clearer.

Intentional Limitations

The following features are not implemented but might come later down the line:

  • Cloud sync - all data is stored on your device only. This would require a lot more security and a subscription model.
  • Google sign-in - also requires more security.
  • Form auto-fill, such as auto-completing an address - paid subscription only.
  • Language support, other than UK and US - complex, especially with tax.
  • Multiple users - might come with cloud sync.
  • Sending quotes from the app directly - all of the above would be required with the caveats.
