Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

App: Quote Forge (package com.redaemor.quoteforge)

Contact: query_forge@outlook.com

Summary

Quote Forge is an offline-first quote follow-up tool for tradespeople. Your business data (customers, jobs, quotes, settings, and attachments) is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export an encrypted backup. We do not operate a hosted sync service in the current version, and your business records are not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Data We Process

Data Purpose Stored where
Customer names, phone numbers, emails, addresses, notes Quote and follow-up management On your device (SQLite)
Job and quote details, line items, amounts Business records On your device
Company logo and quote attachments you add Branding and job files On your device
App settings (VAT, reminders, templates) App configuration On your device
Encrypted backup files you create Data portability between your devices Where you save them (clipboard/file - your control)

Data We Do Not Collect (Current Version)

  • No cloud account or server-side database for your quotes in v1.
  • No advertising or analytics SDKs in the initial free release.
  • No sale of personal data.

Permissions

  • Notifications: Optional reminders for quote follow-ups. You can deny permission; the app still works without scheduled alerts.
  • Storage / files: To pick attachments, logos, and open exported PDFs on your device.

Google Sign-In (Not Active in v1)

Sign-in UI is disabled for launch. If enabled in a future update, Google would provide account identifiers and email for optional identity features only; business data would remain governed by this policy and any updated sync terms.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Delete customers, quotes, or jobs in the app to remove them from local storage (soft-delete in database).
  • Uninstalling the app removes local app data from the device.
  • Encrypted backups you saved elsewhere are your responsibility to delete.

There is no online account in v1, so there is no server-side account deletion URL. If you add cloud sync or sign-in later, you must provide in-app and web-based deletion as required by Google Play.

Children

Quote Forge is a business tool, not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when features change (e.g. ads, sync, sign-in). The in-app link will point to the current version.
